# Survey Instrument

This document presents the full survey instrument used in the study, including
participant instructions, eligibility screening, demographic questions,
main task questions, and post-task evaluation measures.

The survey was administered online using Qualtrics. Question order within
the main task block was randomized as described below.

---

## 1. Welcome and Instructions

💡Welcome! 
You are invited to take part in a human-AI collaboration research study. Our goal is to understand how people evaluate answers and explanations generated by Artificial Intelligence (AI).

This survey takes about 20–30 minutes. No worries! If you do not have enough time right now, you can return later using the same browser to complete the survey. ## 2. Consent

KEY INFORMATION ABOUT THE RESEARCH STUDY
 
Study Purpose: The purpose of this study is to examine how different types of explanations generated by large language models (LLMs) influence human trust and decision-making accuracy in AI-assisted tasks. 

Voluntary Consent: Participation is voluntary, and you have the option to not participate.

Activities and Procedures: Your part in the study will be to complete a brief online task. You will be shown multiple-choice questions along with an LLM-generated explanation for one answer. You will then be asked to evaluate how convincing the explanation is, state whether you agree with the answer, and report your level of confidence.

Participation Time: It will take you about 30 minutes to be in this study.

Risks and Discomforts: We do not anticipate any risks or discomforts from participating in this study. However, participants may experience minor fatigue from reading and evaluating explanations.

Possible Benefits: You may receive a direct benefit in the form of monetary compensation for your time and effort. Participants will receive a $10.00 gift card upon completing the study task. In addition, by participating, you may gain insight into how artificial intelligence (AI) systems generate explanations and influence human reasoning

EXCLUSION/INCLUSION REQUIREMENTS
 
Participants must be 18 years of age or older, located in the United States and be able to read English, and can use a phone or a laptop to complete the survey.

INCENTIVES
Participants will receive compensation of $10 gift card for completing the study task. Full compensation will be provided upon completion of all study activities.
 
PROTECTION OF PRIVACY AND CONFIDENTIALITY
The results of this study may be published in scientific journals, professional publications, or educational presentations. All findings will be reported in aggregated form, and individual participants will not be identifiable in any dissemination of results.

The information collected during the study will not be used or distributed for future research studies. No identifiable information will be collected during the study or on the research study instruments.

CONSENT

By participating in the study, you indicate that you have read the information written above, been allowed to ask any questions, and you are voluntarily choosing to take part in this research. You do not give up any legal rights by taking part in this research study.

## 5. Main Task Instructions

✨ Let’s get started! You will see 40 multiple-choice questions. For each question, please rate your trust in the AI-provided answer and explanation, and provide your reason for the trust.

For each question:
- An AI-selected answer would be shown.
- An AI-generated explanation supporting that answer would be shown.
- Participants were asked to evaluate their trust in the AI-generated explanation
  and provide a reason for their trust judgment.

---

## 6. Main Task Questions

Each main task question followed the same structure.

### Question Template

- **Category:** One of several domains (e.g., Biological/Medical Sciences,
  Mathematical Reasoning, Physical and Engineering Sciences, Social Sciences and Law).
- **Question:** A domain-specific multiple-choice question with four options (A–D).
- **AI-provided answer:** A single answer option selected by the AI.
- **AI-generated explanation:** A textual explanation generated by the AI.

#### Example Question

**Category:** Biological, Medical, and Health Sciences  
**Question:** A pulse may be felt on a patient's face where the  
A. facial artery crosses the mandible  
B. facial artery crosses the maxilla  
C. superficial temporal artery crosses the maxilla  
D. superficial temporal artery crosses the orbital rim  

**AI-provided answer:** [Shown to participant]  
**AI-generated explanation:** [Shown to participant]

---

## 7. Trust Evaluation Measure

After each question, participants answered:

> Please rate how much you trust the AI-generated explanation for this question. Select a number from 1 – 7, where 1 = Not trust at all and 7 = Trust completely. 

Responses were collected on a **7-point Likert scale**:
- 1 = Not trust at all  
- 7 = Trust completely

---

## 8. Trust Attribution Question

After rating trust, participants were asked to answer "Reason why you trust/do not trust the AI-generated result:"

- I am confident in the answer, regardless of the explanation
- The explanation itself is convincing / not convincing
- I trust / do not trust AI models
- Other (free-response)

## 10. Randomization and Presentation Logic

- The order of the 40 main task questions was randomized for each participant.
- Each participant saw exactly one AI-generated explanation per question.
- Explanation content varied across predefined experimental conditions.
- All participants completed the same evaluation and trust measures.

---

## 11. Data Handling and Privacy

- No personally identifiable information was collected.
- Responses were stored and analyzed in aggregate form only.
- Raw participant responses are not publicly released due to ethical and
  institutional review constraints.
